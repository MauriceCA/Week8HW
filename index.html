About Us

Corey and Maurice have been a couple for 5 years, but have been friends for over 15 years! Corey, with a soft spot for S Chassis, has owned at least four 240's. Maurice, not being a person who was super into cars, thought it would be a fun idea and project for both of them to each have a car that they can drive around together and go to car meets in. This started the search for a car for Maurice and eventually lead to them acquiring the 200sx. The couple currently resides in Albuquerque, NM.
