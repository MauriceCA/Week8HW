The Story

In August of 2012, Corey bought his current 240sx in Taos, NM. Minor modifications were made on the car but in April 2020, the water pump on his KA24e engine exploded. Prompting the idea to do an engine swap. Over the course of the COVID-19 pandemic, Corey started and completed his engine swap, repainted his car exterior and engine bay, and put new wheels on the car.
Read the list of modifications that were done to this 240sx below.

The Build

Corey's 240sx has the following modifications:

  • RB20DET engine
  • Walbro 255LPH fuel pump
  • ISR Engine Mounts
  • CX Racing intercooler and piping
  • HKS Blowoff Valve
  • Mishimoto radiator
